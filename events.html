Coach Outings

From time to time the club organises optional coach outings, at additional cost, to notable gardens and horticultural destinations. These trips are open to members and provide an enjoyable opportunity to visit interesting gardens together.

Details of forthcoming outings are shared directly with members at meetings and through club communications.
